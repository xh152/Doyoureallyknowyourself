4. Only 2 weeks are left before your close friends' wedding. How are the preparations going?


One month ago, I chose the saxophonist who will play a medley of our school songs / collected the couple's photo love story / composed a poem / pressed my suit / made appointments with the stylist and makeup master. I prefer to be fully armed.

Why prepare? I'll be having fun and enjoying myself at the party. I'll improvise my wedding speech. The best things happen spontaneously.
